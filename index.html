AUTOWN'25

AACE: Autonomous • AI • Cybersecurity • EV

Powering Innovation Towards a Smarter and Safer Automotive Future.

Vision

To create a collaborative platform where academia, industry, and policymakers converge to discuss the transformative technologies shaping the mobility landscape — from autonomous driving and AI-enabled decision systems to cybersecurity and sustainable electric mobility.

Objectives

  • Advance knowledge and collaboration in autonomous vehicles, AI, cybersecurity, and electric mobility.
  • Explore integration of intelligence, connectivity, and security in next-gen vehicles.
  • Provide hands-on learning through workshops and interactive sessions.

About the Event

AUTOWN’25 brings together academia, industry, and policymakers to explore sessions, and hands-on workshops, participants engage with AI, autonomy, transformative automotive technologies. Through expert talks, technical cybersecurity, and sustainable mobility tools and applications.
